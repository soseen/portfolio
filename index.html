About Me

Having come from a small city near Lublin I'm a graduate of the Military University of Technology in Warsaw in the field of computer science. Throughout these years I have come to the decision to focus on web development and I've been constantly improving my abilities in this regard ever since. Although most of my knowledge comes from my own teachings I have also completed a 3 motnhs course in Front-End Development organised by Akademia 108. I believe that these foundations are sufficient enough for me to apply for the first job in web development and expand my knowledge even further which is the current goal I set for myself.
